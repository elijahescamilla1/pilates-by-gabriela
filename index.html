About

Hi, I'm Gabriela!

I am a comprehensively certified BASI Pilates Instructor specializing in personalized Pilates for athletes, dancers, active adults, and individuals recovering from injury.

My teaching combines precision, hands-on guidance, and evidence-informed movement to help clients build strength, improve performance, and move with confidence.
